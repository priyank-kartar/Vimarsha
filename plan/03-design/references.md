## The founding reference

- **`ref books.mp4` ("ART SPACE")** — the 9.7s clip the whole motion language derives from.
  Take: the depth-stack, the editorial header, the physical hardbacks, "all motion no pages."
  Do NOT take: its neutral cream palette (ours is palette-as-canvas,
  [ADR-005](../00-overview/decision-log.md#adr-005--palette-as-the-canvas-dark-first)) or its
  zero-affordance purity (we add glass controls).
  - Frame-by-frame analysis: [`apple/docs/reference/ref-books-video-analysis.md`](../../apple/docs/reference/ref-books-video-analysis.md)
  - Stills: [`apple/docs/reference/frames/`](../../apple/docs/reference/frames/)
  - Source video: `~/Downloads/ref books.mp4` (not committed; the analysis + frames are the
    durable record)
