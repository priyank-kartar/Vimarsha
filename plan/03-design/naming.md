## The name

**Vimarsha** (Sanskrit **विमर्श**) — reflection, deliberation, critical examination. The
product in one word: you don't just consume the book, you *deliberate* with it (narration →
figures → discussion). Working pronunciation for ASO/marketing: "vi-MAR-sha."

- App Store display name: **Vimarsha** (subtitle carries the explanation — see
  [aso-assets](../07-gtm/aso-assets.md)).
- Bundle ids: `com.vimarsha.apple` (Swift app) · `com.vimarsha.vimarsha` (frozen Flutter
  reference — don't collide).
- The SPEKO name/icons found in ~/Downloads belong to the *other* project (speko repo); not
  related despite the timing.

## Feature naming conventions

- **User-facing features get plain words, not brands:** Notes, Figures, Discuss,
  Conversations. (No "Vimarsha Intelligence™" until marketing demands it.)
- **Discuss** is the conversation feature's name (verb-first, matches the gesture: you
  discuss the passage). "Chat" is banned in UI copy (generic, ungrounded connotation).
- **Memo** internally; **Note** in user-facing copy (Notes list).
- Motion patterns and glass moments keep their grammar names in code/specs/commits
  ([motion-grammar](motion-grammar.md)) but never appear in UI copy.
- Chapter states in copy: *Not narrated yet · Narrating… · Ready · Couldn't narrate* (honest,
  no jargon — principle 10).
